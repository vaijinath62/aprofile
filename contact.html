Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

You can create an account by downloading our mobile app from the App Store or Google Play Store, then following the registration process. You'll need to provide your name, email address, and phone number.

How do I book a service?

Simply open the app, select the service you need, choose your preferred date and time, and select a service provider. You can pay securely through the app and track your booking in real-time.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), debit cards, and digital payment methods like Apple Pay and Google Pay. All payments are processed securely.

How do I cancel or reschedule a booking?

You can cancel or reschedule your booking through the app up to 24 hours before the scheduled service time. Go to "My Bookings" and select the option you need. Refunds are processed according to our cancellation policy.

Are your service providers verified?

Yes, all our service providers undergo a thorough verification process including background checks, identity verification, and skill assessments. We also monitor their performance and customer ratings.

What if I'm not satisfied with the service?

We want you to be completely satisfied. If you're not happy with the service, please contact our customer support within 24 hours. We'll work with you to resolve the issue, including offering refunds or rebooking with a different provider.
